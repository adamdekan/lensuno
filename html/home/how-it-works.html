How it works

LENS UNO is an innovative online marketplace that stands out from traditional intermediate agents in the photography and videography industry.

Our platform is free for everyone to join, whether as a customer or a freelancer.

We believe transparency is the key to building trust and strong relationships between customers and freelancers.

We do not contract photographers or videographers, as most common practices do with a subscription fee.

By facilitating open and direct communication between both sides, we aim to help achieve the best results for everyone.

Using LENS UNO is easy.

Step 1. Describe your need

Choose your location and type of shooting requirement.

Step 2. Explore your options

Browse the portfolios and services in the field of your choice for free until you find the ones that perfectly fit your vision, style and budget.

Step 3. Collaborate

Use free chat to communicate your expectations and requirements directly with the photographer and make a free booking or cancellation with your free user account.
